12) Random Events

If configured, random events may occur at the end of any turn with a probability that is configured at the start of the game. Such events might include:

  • "Tumbling Wind" ... all pieces on the board are flipped over
  • "Blue and Orange Recall" ... both blue pieces and both orange pieces are returned home (blue and orange are just an examples)
  • "Yellow and Purple Sink Holes" ... all pieces on either a yellow hexagon or a purple hexagon are returned home (yellow and purple are just examples)
  • "Hex Slide" ... the coloured hexes on the board slide to new positions, the black hexagons appear to remain stationary
To disable random events configure a probability of 0.
